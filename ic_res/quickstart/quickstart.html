Once the app is running and the Board Jumper has finished loading...

  • Open the Navigation Drawer by pressing the menu key, pressing the nav drawer button top left, or drag from the left edge of the screen to the right

  • If necessary, scroll down, then press "Settings"

  • Then, press "Accounts & Notifications," followed by "Manage Accounts"

  • Press "Add Account..."

  • Enter account info, press "OK"

  • Wait for account to verify, if account does not verify, double check username and password

  • Press Back Key three times to return to main screen

  • Open the Navigation Drawer

  • Open the Account Switcher spinner

  • Press recently added account to log into that account

Note: Once you have an account added, you can click on that account in the Manage Accounts page to remove it, or make it the default account - this will make the app always log into this account when starting the app.
